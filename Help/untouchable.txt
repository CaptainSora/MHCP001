Lottery Help Page

Command: .untouchable    Alias: .u

This is a list of arguments passed in the form
'.untouchable (args) (addl args)'.
Additional arguments can be <mandatory> or [optional].

.untouchable ...    Alias   Details
{no argument}               Displays lottery instructions
rates                       Displays lottery odds
history [userid]    (h)     Displays your lottery history
luck [userid]       (l)     Calculates your luck
leaderboards [num]  (lb)    Displays lottery leaderboards
<guess>                     Plays the lottery
random              (r)     Plays the lottery with a random number
help                        Shows this page

Additional argument info:
[userid]: The user whose information you want to see
            ex. 'CapSora#7528'
[num]   : The specific outcome you want to see leaderboards for
            ex. '3'
<guess> : A six-digit (0-9) guess to play the lottery
            ex. '012345'
